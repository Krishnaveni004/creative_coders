Course Description

Cybersecurity is the protection of internet-connected systems, including hardware, software and data, from cyber-attacks. In a computing context, security comprises cybersecurity and physical security -- both are used by enterprises to protect against unauthorized access to data centers and other computerized systems. Information security, which is designed to maintain the confidentiality, integrity and availability of data, is a subset of cybersecurity.

Why Cyber Security Internship Course?
